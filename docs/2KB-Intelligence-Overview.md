# 2KB Intelligence Platform
### ESPC Lifecycle Management & Owner's Rep Decision Support

---

## The Problem

Energy Savings Performance Contracts are complex, multi-year engagements involving dozens of stakeholders, millions of dollars, and hundreds of tracked deliverables. Today, most Owner's Rep firms manage this process with spreadsheets, shared drives, and email chains — leading to missed deadlines, untracked obligations, and savings that go unverified.

There is no purpose-built software for the ESPC Owner's Rep lifecycle. Until now.

---

## What 2KB Intelligence Does

A single platform that manages the entire ESPC engagement — from initial prospect through final closeout — with built-in intelligence that catches what humans miss.

---

## Platform Capabilities

### Portfolio Command Center
Real-time visibility across every active engagement. Project status by phase, portfolio-level KPIs, risk indicators, and team activity — all in one view.

### Asset Intelligence
Complete equipment inventories with automated condition analysis. The system evaluates each asset and flags compliance risks (refrigerant phase-outs, equipment past useful life, safety concerns), estimates remaining service life, and calculates replacement costs — eliminating manual assessment errors.

### Facility Benchmarking
Utility data analysis with automatic Energy Use Intensity calculations, cost-per-square-foot trending, and capital planning forecasts. Identifies the buildings and systems with the greatest savings opportunity.

### Financial Modeling
ESPC-specific financial analysis including ECM-level cost and savings modeling, Debt Service Coverage Ratio, Net Present Value, and simple payback. Enables rapid evaluation of ESCO financial proposals against industry standards.

### Governance & Accountability
The core of Owner's Rep oversight:
- **Milestone tracking** — every deliverable, every deadline, every responsible party
- **Risk management** — severity scoring, likelihood assessment, mitigation planning
- **Change order documentation** — full audit trail of scope modifications
- **Submittal tracking** — equipment specs, shop drawings, O&M manuals
- **Contract obligation monitoring** — what was promised vs. what's been delivered

### Construction Oversight
Installation progress tracking per energy conservation measure. Inspection findings with automated scope deviation detection — the system alerts when what's being installed doesn't match what was contracted.

### Measurement & Verification
Guaranteed savings vs. actual performance tracking with automated drift detection. Identifies when energy savings begin declining and provides the documentation needed to hold ESCOs accountable to their guarantees.

### Automated Reporting
Report generation with built-in quality assurance workflow (Draft → Review → QA → Approved). Reports pull directly from project data — eliminating the manual process of copying numbers between systems.

### Knowledge Management
Institutional memory that grows with every engagement. Industry benchmarks, lessons learned from past projects, and reusable templates ensure consistency and accelerate onboarding for new team members.

### Client Visibility Portal
A secure, read-only view for client organizations to monitor their project status, milestones, and key metrics — reducing status update meetings and phone calls.

### Intelligent Assistant
Ask questions about any project in plain language. The system queries the full project database and returns answers, flagged issues, and recommended actions — giving engineers instant access to insights that would normally require hours of spreadsheet analysis.

### Document Intelligence
Upload field photos, equipment nameplates, and specification documents. The system automatically extracts structured data — manufacturer, model, year, condition — and populates the asset inventory without manual data entry.

### Seamless Export
Every data view exports to formatted Excel workbooks or CSV files with one click. Multi-sheet exports for complex datasets (governance records, financial models, full project summaries). The platform is the system of record — spreadsheets are the delivery format.
